Open Market Center

shop.cafe24.com

Offering a range of essential features for managing online stores.

Making the online marketplace your own

Shopcafe is a free solution by which you can process everything from placing an order, managing stocks, to handling customer service of products registered in online marketplace in one simple step.
Not only cafe24 Shopping Mall Center users, but all users of the online marketplace can also benefit from our solution.

Upload and manage multiple products on the online marketplace collectively.

No need to upload and manage each product manually.
A unique product code is assigned to each product to make managing orders, customer inquiries and product status more convenient.
With "Shopcafe Gallery", you can also display all products currently for sale on one page and maximize exposure for your main products.

Most owners also have additional online stores in major shopping malls such as Gmarket, Auction, 11st, Interpark and more.
Cafe24 Open Market Center's aim is to allow these owners to run trouble-free online stores with the cafe24 E-Commerce Solution.
