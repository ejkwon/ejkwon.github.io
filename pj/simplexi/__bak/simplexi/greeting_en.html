Message from the CEO

We will strive to provide competitive global service.

SimpleX Internet CEO Lee Jae-Suk

Practical management based on trust
Customers base their satisfaction on how much they can trust a company. Our priority is to satisfy our customers through continuous investment on technology and infrastructure.

Trust among employees, between employees and the company, and between the company and customers is all that makes the difference for a business.

Building trust within an organization works as the foundation for improving competitiveness.

Hence, when trust is incorporated into a company's management philosophy and vision, it creates a synergy effect which eventually leads to the gaining of "customer trust".

"Korean Wave" has been one of the most significant cultural trends of the last decade and I believe now is the perfect opportunity for Korean companies to expand into the global market.

SimpleX Internet will strive to provide competitive global solutions through innovation and investment to help more customers step out into the global market.
