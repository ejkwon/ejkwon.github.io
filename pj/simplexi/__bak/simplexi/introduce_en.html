About Us

Cafe24 is a global success partner and frontiers of the IT industry.

Through continuous investment in technology and infrastructure, and innovative services, we provide customers with global solutions that have been proven to succeed domestically and abroad.

"cafe24 Hosting Center" provides web hosting and server hosting services. "cafe24 Shopping Mall Center" leads the industry by offering free solutions for building domestic and global online shopping malls with ease. "cafe24 Marketing Center" offers online marketing consultations (also working as an Online Marketing Agency) and provides overseas marketing services with its comprehensive analysis system.

SimpleX Internet also provides other offline services including cafe24 Design Center for shopping mall designs, and cafe24 SOHO Business Center for office space and consultation services.
