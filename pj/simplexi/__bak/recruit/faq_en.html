Recruiting FAQs

Below are recruiting-related frequently asked questions and answers.

For other inquiries, please contact us via e-mail (recruit@simplexi.com) for a more prompt response.

Is work experience mandatory?

No. Work experience is not mandatory unless the requirements for the desired position states otherwise.

How can I apply?

Submit your resume, cover letter and other required documents to HR via e-mail (recruit@simplexi.com).
We will review your resume and cover letter in case of a vacancy in the stated position.

You may also visit job recruitment websites such as Job Korea, etc. for information on job positions.

I'm interested in more than one position. Can I apply for all of them?

We will take this into consideration if you list all the desired positions within our online application form.

There is nothing about age in the job requirement. Are there no age limits?

No. All of our positions do not have an age limit for the applicants.

How do I dress for the interview?

There is no specific dresscode for the interview. We recommend that you wear something comfortable and casual.

When do we get results regarding the paper-selection process and interview?

We will individually contact applicants who have passed the paper-selection process. Applicants who are set for an interview will be contacted individually via telephone and e-mail.

Can I receive military service-related exceptions when applying?

No. We at SimpleX Internet do not accept any transferral of Skilled Industry Personnel.

Job openings for Technical Research Personnel may change depending on the decision by the Military Manpower Administration, but we initially do not accept any transferral of Skilled Industry or Technical Research Personnel.

How can I check if my application was submitted successfully?

Submission is successful f there are no error messages upon submitting your application online.
Submission may appear to be unsuccessful due to individual Microsoft Outlook settings, but all submitted application forms are being carefully reviewed.

HR will check the application forms within 3 days of submission and applicants who have passed the paper-selection process will be contacted via telephone and e-mail.

How do make other inquiries?

Please contact us at recruit@simplexi.com and we will get back to you promptly.
