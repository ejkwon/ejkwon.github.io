Benefits and Perks

Employees' happiness and wellbeing are our top priorities.

Happy Workplace

We offer comprehensive compensation and benefits packages to empower employees with the resources,
incentives, and flexibility necessary to enjoy success at work and to live a healthy, balanced life.

  • Working Hours 5 days a week (9am-6pm, 40 hours a week), including paid vacations.
  • Flexible Salary We promote a very flexible, performance-related salary system for our employees.
  • Time Off Every fourth Friday of the month is Leisure Day when employees can take time off to relax and recharge.
    Leisure Day also comes with a 100,000 Korean Won allowance to let employees enjoy more and worry less.
  • Annual Welfare Points We provide 800,000 Welfare Points annually for employees to use freely for their life and wellbeing.
  • Work-related Resources Employees can purchase approved work-related resources such as books, etc., and share them with others.
  • Meal Allowance We provide a daily meal (lunch) allowance of 7,000 Korean Won for employees.
  • Health Coverage We provide medical check-ups in association with the country's leading medical center for the health and wellness of our employees.
  • Paid Leaves We provide 30 paid leave days for employees who have worked for a full 7 years.
