Recruiting Process

Selection Process

  1. Apply
  2. Paper Selection : Examining essential requirements, career and reason for application
  3. Character Interview : By HR Team
  4. Official Interview : By Team Leader
  5. CEO Interview : By CEO and the Board
  6. Final Decision
