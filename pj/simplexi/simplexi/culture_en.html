Our Corporate Culture

Creating a corporate culture based on employees' creativity and passion.

  • Horizontal organization
    Discussing and sharing ideas Based on a non-hierarchical structure, we promote discussing and sharing of ideas between employees.

  • Addressing each other
    Communicating freely, but with respect Employees at cafe24 respect each other.
    The horizontal structure allows them to address each other freely by their names, but with respect, regardless of age or position.

  • The more you grow, the more you earn
    Individual ability-based salaries We, at cafe24, believe that salaries don't commensurate with position or the years of experience, but with individual ability and the potential to grow.

  • Casual dress code
    Dress freely No need to suit up. From casual to formal, employees are encouraged to dress to their own liking.

  • Leisure activities
    Every fourth Friday is "Leisure Day" Here at cafe24, every fourth Friday is "Leisure Day" where 100,000 Korean Won allowance is provided for employees to use for various purposes. We value the balance between life and work, so take a day off and enjoy with family and friends.
